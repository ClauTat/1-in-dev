About the Library

The City Library provides free access to books, digital resources, and community programs. Our mission is to make knowledge and culture available to everyone.

  • Open to all ages
  • Free membership
  • Accessible facilities and resources

Collections

We offer a variety of collections tailored to different interests and needs. Below are featured categories:

  1. Children & Young Adult — books, audiobooks, and early literacy programs.
  2. Research & Reference — journals, databases, and local history archives.
  3. Digital Media — e-books, streaming music, and educational videos.

Events & Programs

Regular events include storytime, digital skills workshops, and community talks. Visit the events page or contact us to learn more.
